Filters can be applied to see only selected data from a file.

head - shows the top few lines of the file.
tail - shows the last few lines of the file.
sort - sorts the lines. default: alphabetically based on the first alphabet of the line.
nl - inserts line numbers.
wc - gives the count of words/charecters/lines (based on requirement) .
cut - divides the data based on seperatror charecter.
sed - seacrh and replac.
uniq - removes duplicate lines.
tac - prits last line through first line.
